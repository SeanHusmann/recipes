Ingredients:
 - 1/2 teaspoon sugar
 - 1 stick Jacobs Espresso instant coffee powder
 - 50 ml hot water
 - 250 ml milk

Directions:
 1. Add sugar and instant coffee powder to 300 ml cup/glass.
 2. Boil some water with a water cooker or pot.
 3. Add 50 ml of hot water to cup.
 4. Gently shake cup in a rotational motion to smoothly blend ingredients for 5 seconds.
 5. Add 250 ml of milk, cold, warm or hot.
